# AI Models Comparison

## 1. Runway

### Pros
- High-quality videos
- Easy-to-use interface
- Powerful Gen-4 models

### Cons
- Paid plans
- API access is limited

### Pricing
Free trial available.
Paid plans start around $15/month.

### Ease of Integration
Easy using API.

---

## 2. Pika

### Pros
- Fast generation
- Beginner friendly

### Cons
- Limited customization
- Watermark in free version

### Pricing
Free + Paid plans

### Ease of Integration
Moderate.

---

## 3. Luma

### Pros
- Excellent realistic videos
- Good motion quality

### Cons
- Premium features require subscription

### Pricing
Free and paid plans

### Ease of Integration
Easy.

---

## 4. Stable Video Diffusion

### Pros
- Open source
- Runs locally
- Highly customizable

### Cons
- Needs a powerful GPU
- More difficult to set up

### Pricing
Free

### Ease of Integration
Moderate to difficult.
